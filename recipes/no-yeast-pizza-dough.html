No-Yeast Pizza Dough

Here's how to make the perfect no-yeast pizza dough with these simple steps.

Ingredients:

  • Flour;
  • Baking powder;
  • A pinch of salt;
  • Half-cup of fat-free milk;
  • Olive oil.

Steps:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, then stir in the wet ingredients until a dough forms;
  2. Turn out the dough and knead;
  3. Shape the dough into a ball and let rise;
  4. Roll the dough out into a circle.

How to cook the pizza:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C);
  2. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 8 minutes;
  3. Top with your favorite toppings and bake until the crust is golden brown, about 10 to 15 minutes more;
